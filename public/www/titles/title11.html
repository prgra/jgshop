title definition   


"Master Bounty Hunter"
 
Small Trophy
this title is only awarded to bounty hunters of the highest caliber. the recipient of such an award is currently receiving the highest average bounty per hour. since bounty is scaled dependant on the value of each ship, this award signifies that this pilot is likely pitting himself against pilots of considerably superior means, tech, and weaponry. this pilot is also consistently engaging in combat.

"TRI awards you with this title for superlative and exemplary combat service. you are hereby granted all the rights and privileges associated with this award."
