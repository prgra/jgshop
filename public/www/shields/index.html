Shields   


whoever said dog is man's best friend obviously never had to fly a 100 cubic meter shipment of uranium into a hotzone on the butthole of a solar hurricane, where the only thing standing between you and what used to be you is your true best friend... your shield generator.

pick your shield generator wisely. too heavy, and you're as evasive as cold molasses. too small, a virgin on his wedding night will last longer than you.
