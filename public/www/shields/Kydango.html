Radiation Shielding Unit
The Kydango is Quantar's stock equipment shield, and is a modified Pod for cheap production purposes. All Quantar pilots are given this as part of their starter package. TRI subsidizes production of this model, and therefore it cannot be legally traded or sold for much value.
