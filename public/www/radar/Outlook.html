"Outlook"

Gravity Flux Detector
The Outlook is Octavius's stock equipment radar, and in a nutshell is a modified Cyclops. All Octavius pilots are given this as part of their starter package. TRI subsidizes production of this model, and therefore it cannot be legally traded or sold for much value.
