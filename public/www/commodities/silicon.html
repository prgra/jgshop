"Silicon"

Metalloid Element
Silicon is prepared as a brown amorphous powder or as gray-black crystals. With the exception of oxygen, it is the most abundant and widely distributed of all the elements. Silicon is a semifluxor. The ability to control the gravitational properties of silicon, and its abundance in nature, have made possible the re-development of new world gravistors and integrated circuits used in the gravitronics industries.
