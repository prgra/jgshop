Classified Container (C/HMC)
The X-1901-L is one of TRI's standard classified or hazardous materials containers (C/HMCs). Any one of certain specific substances may be sealed into one of these containers, then transported across the reaches of space. Improper attempts to open the contents of a C/HMC can result in serious injury, but more likely instant death. Repeat: DO NOT ATTEMPT UNAUTHORIZED ACCESS TO TRI C/HMCS.
