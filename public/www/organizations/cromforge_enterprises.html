Cromforge Enterprises has powered the galaxy since the recovery from the Great Collapse. Having a huge advantage due to its close relationship to The Reconstruction Initiative, Cromforge leapt ahead quickly. Thanks to the relentless pursuit by arch-competitor Aristio Conglomerate, Cromforge has recently moved into the radar market, hoping to diversify their product line. Cromforge is the official producer for the Quantar system.
