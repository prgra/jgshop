C-0 "Jellyfish"

image not to scale
Scouting Drone
The "Jellyfish" class drone is a rather recent discovery for TRI. This class of Conflux seems to be an expendable patrol unit, deployed along side the C-1s. "Jellyfish" do not put up much of a fight, but seem to have advanced "feelers" or antennae, which suggests that they are collecting new data about TRI space for the Conflux collective.
