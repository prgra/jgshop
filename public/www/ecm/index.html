ECMs (electronic counter measures)   


the balance of technology is defined by the increasingly powerful weapons dissuaded by increasingly powerful shields. countermeasures divide the balance. with appropriate devices in appropriate situations, the weapon/shield battle can be turned in your favor despite having the short end of the technology stick. use them wisely, but do not rely on them.
