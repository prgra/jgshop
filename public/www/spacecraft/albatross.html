Utility Shuttle
Working from the basic Apteryx design, Octavian engineers made some trade-offs in order to bring to market a relatively inexpensive craft intended to help further the economic development of the Octavian systems. The Albatross provides its pilot with a useful cargo load at the expense of some combat power.
