Medal Definition

"Echoes of Hope"
 
Prestigious Honors
Director Echo Tetsab from TRI's Recreation and Morale Department designed this award specifically to be given to pilots who organize activities or provide other services for the express purpose of recreation or morale boosting to their fellow pilots.

"We are delighted to award this medal on behalf of TRI Recreation and Morale to pilots who serve mostly to benefit others. You are hereby granted all the rights and privileges associated with this award."
