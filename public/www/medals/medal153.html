Medal Definition

"Medal of Hermod"
 
Prestigious Honors
This medal is awarded to expeditious pilots who have overcome all odds (and opponents) during the Great Space Race. This medal is awarded to the top pilot in each faction and rank bracket. (1st through 5th level pilots, 6th through 15th level pilots, 16th through 25th level pilots, 25th and up pilots)

"TRI awards you with this medal for pernicity and swiftness. This medal is a badge of honor which should be worn with pride, as few are the pilots who earn them."
