Medal Definition

"Solrain Demerit Ribbon"
 
Military Demerit
This ribbon is assigned to pilots who perform dishonorable actions or disobey Rules of Engagement while in the Solrain Military service. Successive assignment of this token will result in dishonorable discharge for actions unbecoming a Flight Officer.


"The Solrain Traders and Citizens' Council hereby orders you to display this mark of shame until you are able to redeem yourself by your actions. Your pay will be docked and your opportunities for promotion will be reduced until you have earned its removal."
