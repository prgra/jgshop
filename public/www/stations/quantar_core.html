"Quantar Core"

image not to scale
Delta Class
Quantar Core station was built inside a particularly dense field of asteroids near the Quantar home-planet of Quanus. Over the years, this field of asteroids has been wholly mined out, forcing pilots to venture into nearby systems. This station also serves as an orbital refinery.
