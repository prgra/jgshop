"Amananth"

image not to scale
Charlie Class
Like all Amananth technology, little is known about this station. Pilots brave enough to dock at Amananth report it to be devoid of life, but miraculously clean and well maintained. Every door within this station, save those to the docking bays, is electro-magnetically sealed.
