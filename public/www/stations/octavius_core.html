"Octavius Core"

image not to scale
Delta Class
Octavius Core is the most heavily shielded station in the known universe. Octavius Core is well known for its efficient flight deck coordinators, who are rumored to have scrambled and launched eight separate wings of fighters in under 13 minutes.
