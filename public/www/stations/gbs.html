Derelict Relic
GBS, which was discovered adrift near the pulsar, was by far the most heavily damaged of the derelict stations. 3 TRI building crews were deployed, and over 6 months were spent on its restoration. GBS station is currently held together by over 4000 pneumatic support brackets.
