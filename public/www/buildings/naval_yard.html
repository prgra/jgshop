"Naval Yard"
Spacecraft Development Facility
This building is used for the design and testing of new ships. Flying through the access tunnel of this building has no effect upon your ship. It is simply used for cargo trasnfers with the primary station.

Building Specifications:
This facility is the most advanced building available at present. This structure can be used for a wide range of high-stess prototype spacecraft tests. Once this facility has been built, the adjacent station will be able to produce new types of spacecraft. (These craft are faction specific.)

The docking port on this building is used for personnel and equipment transferal only.
