"Science Factory"
Free-Floating Factory
The Science Factory researches and produces new complex equipment. While primarily a research facility, this building also serves as a factory for the researched gear.

Building Specifications:
With the changes which the Great Collapse made to the known physics of the universe, the properties and interaction of elements in space are constantly being researched. The Science Factory was created specifically as a sterile, sanitary environment for research and production of high-tech equipment. This building houses manufacturing facilities to mass produce newly discovered or created equipment.

Once you have targeted a completed Science Factory, you will notice two lines of text next to its radar display. The first line contains the name of the building, and what it produces. (Example: Science Factory:Composites*) The second line contains a list of commodities which are required to complete the transaction. You will only need one unit of each. (Example: Al, Cr, Ir, Fe)

*Composites used as an example only. Science Factories do not produce common commodities
