"Refueling Stop"
Deep Space Fuel Container
Stopping at this building will refuel your booster and braking fuel. These buildings can be found throughout space.

Building Specifications:
Due to the limited fuel space available in single pilot craft, TRI has commissioned the construction of deep space fueling stops. This building is essentially a free floating fuel tank.

Pilots will be charged a nominal refueling fee for using this structure.
