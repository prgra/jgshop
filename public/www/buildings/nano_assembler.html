"Nano Assembler"
Particle Collection Facility
This building uses magnetic fields and nano-technology to reclaim specific commodities from ions and particles adrift in space. You may purchase this commodity directly from this facility.

Building Specifications:
Ships and equipment are destroyed every day, leaving debris and refuse throughout space. Through the use of nanites (microscopic robots) this waste can be reclaimed. These nanites are programed to seek and reclaim a specific commodity from the debris piles which drift by or are are routinely dropped off at this facility.

Pilots may dock at a Nano Assembler, and purchase the reclaimed commodity for the going retail price. Once you have targeted a completed Nano Assembler, you will notice several pieces of data next to its radar display. The first piece of data is the commodity which this Assembler produces, and the second is the retail price of the commodity. (Example: Nano Assembler: Water 1725)
