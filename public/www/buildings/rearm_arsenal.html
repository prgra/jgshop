"Rearm Arsenal"
Ammunition Dump
Stopping at this building will rearm your ammunition based weapons. These buildings can be found throughout space.

Building Specifications:
With the rise of pirates and terrorists, TRI has decided to deploy ammunition stops at strategic point throughout space. These buildings will refill ammunition pods for pilots who choose to use ammo based weapons.

Pilots will be charged a nominal rearmament fee for using this structure.
