"Custom Producer"
Free-Floating Factory
This building is used to produce a specific type of commodity or equipment. If the required commodities are present in your cargo bay, they will be removed and replaced with this item.

Building Specifications:
The custom production facility is a self-contained factory that produces a specific piece of equipment or commodity. This facility is constantly producing this device, and is in constant need of raw materials. By docking at this station with these materials, you will initiate a trade - your commodities for the station's product.

Once you have targeted a completed Custom producer, you will notice two lines of text next to its radar display. The first line contains the name of the building, and what it produces. (Example: Custom Producer:Dream) The second line contains a list of commodities which are required to complete the transaction. You will only need one unit of each. (Example: Fe, Ti, FU, GravC)
