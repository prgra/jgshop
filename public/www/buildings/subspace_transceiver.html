"Subspace Transceiver"
Long Range Radio Tower
This tower allows broadcasts to be made to pilots anywhere in known space. Strategically this is a very important structure, and can be helpful in organizing faction-wide tasks such as combat against conflux war parties, or hunting down wanted criminals.







Building Specifications:
It has been said that communication is the key to completing any task. The Subspace Transceiver was designed to fill this void, and allows a faction's command center to more easily communicate the needs of the faction to their agents. These buildings are often used to coordinate raids against the conflux, organize pirate manhunts, and inform traders of commodity shortages.
