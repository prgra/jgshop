"Repair Station"
Deep Space Repair Facility
Stopping at this building will repair your hull damage. These buildings can be found throughout space.

Building Specifications:
The Repair Station, like the Refueling Stop, is a deep space support building. Through the use of built in damage detection fields, this automated structure assesses the armor level of a ship, and applies armor patches accordingly.

Pilots will be charged a nominal repair fee for using this structure.
